Τhis spacious villa is set in Steni Vala, a small marina. Glyfa Beach is within 250 meters. The villa is surrounded by wide balconies with views of Kyra Panagia and Peristera islands. The house has 4 bedrooms and 3 bathrooms with showers. A fully equipped kitchen with 2 refrigerators and a freezer, a dishwasher, an oven and a microwave is available. The living room features a traditional fireplace and chestnut wood ceiling, a satellite TV, DVD, CD player and radio. The house is surrounded by a lush garden, with olive trees, lemon trees, orange trees, cherry trees and fig trees.
